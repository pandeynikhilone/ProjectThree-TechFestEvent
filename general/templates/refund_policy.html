Refund and Cancellation Policy for Techfest 4.0

We want to ensure a smooth and transparent process for all participants of Techfest 4.0. Below is our Refund and Cancellation Policy:

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds will be provided under the following conditions:

Event Cancellation: If the event is cancelled by the organizers, participants will be eligible for a full refund.

Refunds Not Allowed: Refund requests will not be processed once the participant has received the admit card.

2. Refund Process

Participants wishing to request a refund must follow these steps:

How to Request: Refund requests should be submitted via email or phone.

Required Information: Participants need to provide the following details for a refund request:

Transaction ID

Participant Name

Contact Details

Event Name

Refund Processing Time: Refund requests will be processed within 7-8 working days and credited to the participantâ€™s bank account.

3. Cancellation Rules

Cancellation by Participant: Participants are not allowed to cancel their registration once submitted, except in the case of event cancellation by the organizers.

Exceptions: There are no exceptions for cancellations, including medical emergencies.

4. Mode of Refund

Refunds will be processed using the original payment method, which may include:

Razorpay (if used for payment)

UPI (if the payment was made via UPI)

5. Event Cancellation by Organizers

In Case of Cancellation or Rescheduling: If Techfest 4.0 is cancelled or rescheduled by the organizers, all participants will be eligible for a full refund.

Full Refund Assurance: A full refund will be credited if the event is cancelled by the organizers.

6. Contact Information

For any inquiries regarding refunds or cancellations, please contact us via the Contact Us page

We hope this policy helps clarify any questions you may have. Thank you for participating in Techfest 4.0!
